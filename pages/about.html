Ocean Gardens


The ocean is one of nature's greatest mysteries. Over the years, decades, centuries, the exploration of this incredible ecosystem has always been an enigma, given the vastness and depth of the oceans and seas.


“Ocean Gardens” is a Nasa Space Apps Challenge proposal that challenges us to create an accessible platform that visually, sonically, and interactively educates users about important ocean-provided services in a way they can easily understand. In order to reach that purpose, our team decided to make an interactive and immersive website that, in an attractive way, explains the different aspects in which the oceans impact and are important to our daily lives.

That way, our main goal is to spread awareness to as many people as we can, in a way that they make more conscious decisions related to the topic in the future. Therefore, we, as a population, can protect the ocean and, consequently, the environment as a whole.
