Ocean Garden

Oceans cover more than 70% of the surface of the Earth. They hold the world’s biggest ecosystem and contain 94% of the world’s wildlife. They also contain 97% of Earth’s water. Besides that, less than 5% of the ocean has been explored.


Most people don’t recognize the importance of the oceans and how much they impact our daily lives and all forms of life on the planet. We can compare the oceans to big diverse gardens that provide us with food, a stable atmosphere, diverse life forms and beauty in many different ways.
